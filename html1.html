Portland Beer Events

Portland is not shy about its love of beer. The the city has nearly as many beer festivals as it does breweries. And with beer festivals all over the calendar, the city has thirst-quenching events year-round. Belly up to some of Beervana's biggest annual suds celebrations.

Portland Beer Week is a celebration of the local craft brewing scene, the 11-day Portland Beer Week (because seven days cannot contain Portland beer) is packed full of tastings, festivals, parties and educational events all over town.

This website highlights the annual, recurring beer and cider festivals that take place in Portland. It is our goal to be a helpful resource for those interested in the awesome beer festivals that happen here in Portland.
